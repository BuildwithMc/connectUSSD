Test Phase Learnings

Built from real user feedback.

‚úì What Worked

The offline USSD interface was a massive success. 100% of test users preferred it over a mobile app ‚Äî eliminating friction from poor internet connectivity entirely.

‚ö° What Surprised Us

Users were alienated by kWh metrics. Selling "Kilowatt-hours" created confusion and suspicion. People think in Naira and hours of light ‚Äî not technical units.

üîÑ The Iteration

We updated the USSD to show only "Estimated Hours of Light" and "Naira Balance." Instantly boosted trust and willingness to pay.
